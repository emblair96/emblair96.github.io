About Me

  • Background
    I am leveraging my creative thinking and problem-solving abilities to build innovative software. Currently a student, I will graduate with a certificate in Full Stack Web Development from Northwestern in March 2021.
  • Why Software Development
    A recent graduate, I started a position in higher education (a field I thought I wanted to pursue long-term). A few months into the role, I rarely felt challenged and felt lost on next steps. Using the extra time during COVID-19, I took a few free courses and knew this was the career I wanted. Coding brings my love of puzzles, desire to be challenged, and creative-thinking capacity into one space. While I wish I would have discovered this sooner, I'm excited to see myself grow as a software developer.
  • Women in Tech
    I have always had a passion for helping women develop a sense of confidence. My Boot Camp instructor, Lori Culberson, has been an amazing female role-model in this aspect. As I learn and grow as a developer, I hope that one day I can be a mentor for women entering the field as Lori has been for me.
