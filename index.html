My approach

  • Functionality
    My coding bootcamp instructor always reminded us to put functionality first. If you have a pretty app that doesn't do anything, there's not much use to it, is there? This is why I put functionality first. The app needs to work and work right.
  • Collaboration
    One of my favorite aspects of web development is the inherhent collaborative nature of the field. Working as a group or team allows us all to bring our various strengths together to build something that wouldn't have been possible alone.
  • User Experience
    I believe web development should always be approached with the user in mind. Will the added feature be intuitive? Easy to nagivate? Useful? These are the questions I ask myself when building an app.
